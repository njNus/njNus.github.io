An education platform with an admin panel for managing teachers and students.

- Profile management for both students and teachers.
- **Two-way attendance:** teachers mark attendance via radio-button selection; students self-record via OTP verification.
- **Stack:** HTML5, CSS, PHP, JavaScript, MySQL, XAMPP.
