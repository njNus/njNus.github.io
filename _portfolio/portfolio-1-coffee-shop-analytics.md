An interactive **Streamlit** dashboard analyzing sales across multiple coffee-shop branches (Jan–Jun 2023).

- Dynamic filters (date range, store location, product category) for on-the-fly exploration.
- KPI panels: total revenue, quantity sold, top-performing products, and location-based trends.
- **Stack:** Streamlit, Pandas, Plotly, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Altair, Pillow.
