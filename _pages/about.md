I am an Information and Communication Engineer (B.Sc., [Bangladesh University of Professionals](https://bup.edu.bd/), 2025) working at the intersection of **interpretable machine learning, natural language processing, and generative AI for clinical and public-health decision-making**.

My research develops transformer-based systems that are both clinically grounded and deployable. My undergraduate thesis introduced a consensus-driven, dual-model pseudo-labeling pipeline that produced one of the largest clinically aligned suicide-risk datasets to date — 85,451 r/SuicideWatch posts annotated with C-SSRS-mapped four-tier risk labels — and fine-tuned BERT/DistilBERT/RoBERTa for real-time risk stratification. More broadly, I am interested in mechanistic interpretability of language and diffusion models, and in closing the gap between diagnostic explainability and reliable clinical deployment.

## Research interests

- Interpretable AI for critical decision-making
- Natural Language Processing
- Diffusion interpretability
- Generative AI & Large Language Models (LLMs) in clinical practice

## Selected highlights

- **5 peer-reviewed conference papers** (ICCIT 2024/2025, STI 2025) on depression detection, cyberbullying detection, software-requirement classification, and mental-health prediction.
- **Undergraduate thesis** on transformer-based suicide-risk classification using a clinically validated, custom-generated dataset.
- Software-engineering experience in production microservices (Spring Boot/Cloud, PostgreSQL, Kafka, Redis) and computer-vision biometrics (facial recognition, OCR, fingerprint processing).
